PLANT: Mint

Attributes:
 - family 'string'
 - height(ft) integer
 - root_depth(ft) integer
 - light_tolerance 'string'
 - isOutdoor/Indoor boolean

 Methods:
 - grow affects height
 - water affects root_depth
 - prune affects height
 - transplant affects isoutdoor/indoor

CHILD: Cousin Dominque

Attributes:
- date_of_birth integer
- parents 'string'
- weight integer
- isfemale boolean
- address 'string'

Methods:
- born affects Date_of_birth
- adopted affects parents (changes to biological or adopted)
- eat affects weight
- move affects address
- self_identify affect isfemale
- calculate_age (subtracts current year from date_of_birth )

BOAT: Sailboat

Attributes:
- current_number_of_persons_aboard integer
- color 'string'
- speed integer
- sail_quantiy integer
- gas_tank boolean

Methods:
- reserve affects current_number_of_persons_aboard (subtract max_number_of_persons_aboard from current_number_of_persons_aboard )
- speed affected by sail_quantiy
- paint affects color attribute
- refill affects gas_tank

DOG: Wolf

Attributes:
- pet boolean
- fur_color 'string'
- ishappiness boolean
- bladder_fullness
- current_bladder_capacity

Methods:
- get_older (increase age, fur_color will change at 'x' number)
- play affects happiness
- drink (adds amount to current_bladder_capacity, must stop at bladder_fullness)
- go_outside affects current_bladder_capacity (bladder_capacity sets to 0%)
